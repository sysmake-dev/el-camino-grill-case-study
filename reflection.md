# El Camino Grill — Reflection

## REFLECTION

El Camino Grill was my first complete web development case study and served as the foundation for the projects that followed.

The objective was to design and develop a clean, mobile-first one-page website for a local family-owned Mexican food truck. Instead of focusing on visual complexity, the project prioritized clear information hierarchy, accessibility, and an intuitive user experience that allows visitors to quickly find essential information and take action whether indoors or outdoors.

## WHAT WENT WELL

The planning process established a strong foundation for the project. Creating a wireframe before development made it easier to organize content and maintain a clear visual hierarchy throughout implementation.

The mobile-first approach also proved successful. Navigation remains simple and intuitive across screen sizes, while interactive components such as the hamburger menu and expandable menu section provide additional functionality without overwhelming the interface.

Accessibility was another strong outcome. Using semantic HTML, keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus states, and appropriate ARIA attributes helped ensure the website remained usable for a broader range of visitors.

## WHAT I LEARNED

This project reinforced that an effective website is not defined by the number of features it contains, but by how clearly it solves a user's problem.

For a small food truck business, visitors typically want to answer a few simple questions:

- What food is available?
- Where is the business located?
- How can I contact them?

Designing around these questions resulted in a much clearer and more effective user experience than attempting to include unnecessary features or visual complexity.

## KEY TAKEAWAYS

- Careful planning simplifies development.
- Mobile-first design should guide the overall user experience.
- Accessibility should be considered throughout development rather than added afterward.
- Clear information hierarchy improves usability.
- Simple, focused websites often provide the best experience for small local businesses.

## CLOSING

This case study established the foundation for my approach to web development by emphasizing clarity, accessibility, and purposeful design. The lessons learned throughout this project directly influenced the structure, planning process, and implementation of the case studies that followed.
